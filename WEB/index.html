Our Company

We provide artworks whose reliefs allow colors to be distinguished through different sensations.

We want the blind, people through their hands and tactile information to remember and enjoy what a work is like. Like the Roman Colosseum, La Gioconda or El Grito de Munch in volume, playing with textures, sensations, and with the contour of figures.

Our Project

Through 3D print, we will raise awareness amongsighted children about those who cannot see, and the whole society about the importance of the inclusion of blind people in the world of museums, art, culture
